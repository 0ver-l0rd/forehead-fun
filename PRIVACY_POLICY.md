# Privacy Policy for Forehead Fun: The Party Game

**Last updated:** March 11, 2026

Forehead Fun: The Party Game ("we", "our", "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

**We do not collect, store, or share any personal data.** No account creation, login, or registration is required to use the app.

## Third-Party Services

The app uses the **OpenAI API** solely to generate game content (character names and descriptions) based on theme prompts you type. Only the text of your theme prompt is sent to OpenAI — no personal information, device identifiers, or location data is transmitted.

For more information on how OpenAI handles data, please refer to [OpenAI's Privacy Policy](https://openai.com/privacy).

## Network Usage

The app accesses the internet for the following purposes only:

- **Fetching character images** from public sources (Wikipedia, Jikan API)
- **Generating AI character packs** via the OpenAI API when you use the AI Power Pack feature

## Data Storage

The app does not store any data on external servers. All game state (room codes, selected categories, shown characters) exists only in your device's memory during a session and is cleared when you close the app.

## Analytics & Tracking

The app does **not** use any analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs.

## Advertising

The app contains **no advertisements** of any kind.

## Children's Privacy

The app is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with information, please contact us and we will delete it.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
